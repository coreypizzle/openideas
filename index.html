Welcome to OpenIdeas

Imagine a world where you shape the web

This was once an idea...

Just like what you plan to publish on this website, This was an idea.

Just like most great ideas, I thought of it whilst sitting on my phone looking at the latest web & CSS trends.

I though to myself, who makes these trends you know, trends. It was us.

But of course, not all agree! Some say that one high-up person makes it and the whole world copies, sort of like celebrities, they wear skinny jeans, we wear skinny jeans.

Some people aren't the best role models, just because they like something doesn't mean that you do to.

Ok sure, but what does this website have to do with it?

Well, everything!

This website is designed so that any member of the community can help others with their website or app ideas and shape the future of the web in general.

For example, I might think of an idea and find it absolutely amazing but others might not! I would quite like to be prepared for people booing and jeering rather than be all proud and then someone pull me down and tell me it's rubbish.

On this site you upload concepts of your website! The community (other users like you) then decide whether or not they like it or not. They can provide constructive-criticism in the form of comments and vote!

But remember...

Ideas are meant to be built upon. Like the foundation of a building, an idea only is a baseline. Soon creativity will take over and make a dream into a reality. Though the idea can only be built on with support. Like the scaffolding of a building, the community supports the idea and helps it grow. Without the community, ideas would be but a thought.
